21/07/2026 • Aaron-l

First, let's start with general infos: Komica is a 2005 imageboard for Taiwanesse people like Futaba Channel (2chan). Here the users are anonymous and many original characters were born from random posts and inside jokes. 3886 is one of those characters : she comes from a message on the Moe board on June 3, 2006. I always thought that the original thread was lost but while randomly browsing archive of a traditional chinese websites, I unexpectedly managed to find the original discussion. On the early internet Komica, they had their own lore of island full of lolikon girls and original characters, it would be an erotic theme between the few users there were before the website gained any popularity. 3886 is one of those islander, she represents a child-luring character with a very cute caring big sister figure.
