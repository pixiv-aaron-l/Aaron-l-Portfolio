Zenith Lee archive

21/07/2026 • Aaron-l

Hi! This post is about my favourite artist.

First, let's start with general infos : His usernames are ZenithLoveNPeace, S. Zenith Lee, or simply Zenith, and he is a Taiwanese artist on the internet. Many of his works are very related to a 2005 imageboard called Komica. He was drawing for a long time, with his earliest archived piece being from December 16, 2001. He has also been on many other imageboards like guro-chan, 4chan, et cetera... but more importantly, he had websites. I will give you the best links to browse his artworks, as those websites are too old and their archives are trash due to the early internet codes he used to host his works. There are more than 500 amazing masterpieces. All the drawings he did are insane, and I love them so much. Not a lot of them are on booru sites, sadly. So now, let me give you a bunch of useful links:

https://web.archive.org/web/20140424074628/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/index.php - this is his art gallery before quitting.

https://web.archive.org/web/20080327173328/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/index.php - this is the earliest version of his art gallery.

https://web.archive.org/web/20080427013021/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/thumbnails.php?album=1 - album 1, part 1, his beginning here in 2001.

https://web.archive.org/web/20180220000647/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/thumbnails.php?album=1 - album 1, part 2, up to 2003. He named them "very old pictures".

https://web.archive.org/web/20140425133601/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/thumbnails.php?album=2 - album 2. The 2003 works are "old pictures", already high-level.

https://web.archive.org/web/20130223065154/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/thumbnails.php?album=3 - album 3, from 2006 to 2012, is dedicated to the characters from 東方雲夢譚 (河圖-羅森), which is an original work he did. The art is very enjoyable.

https://web.archive.org/web/20080427013031/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/thumbnails.php?album=3 - the other part of album 3. You will also see lots of amazing covers in there.

https://web.archive.org/web/20090508120238/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/thumbnails.php?album=4 https://web.archive.org/web/20081007085453/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/thumbnails.php?album=4 https://web.archive.org/web/20130223065159/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/thumbnails.php?album=4 - this is the 4th album dedicated to 阿里布達年代祭 (河圖-弄玉), and it dates from 2005 to 2012. Masterpieces as always, and it is actually a series of 54 books he has illustrated like album 3. If you type the title on the internet, you will see many people selling it, but sadly, I cannot discover what it is about at the time of this post. I am so sad that I cannot see more of his works and universe yet.

https://web.archive.org/web/20180220000539/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/thumbnails.php?album=5 - and now we go back in time with album 5, from 2004 to 2006. Those are his illustrations of 龍使 (河圖-閒來無事).

https://web.archive.org/web/20180220002213/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/thumbnails.php?album=6 - album 6 in 2004 has a few fan-art pieces for a 1999 visual novel called 秋之回憶. I find Zenith's drawings amazing; it is such an inspiration to me.

- album 7 is called "car and car picture" and had 60 files in 2008 and 7 more in 2010, but we cannot access this content because it is hidden for "adult content". Dumb when you know that other albums have adult content... I know the password was meant to be "YES" because Zenith did not hide his art. In fact, the username and password of the other site we will see way later are just "gurofriend" and "enter", but the Wayback Machine (internet archive tool) was not able to pass the password system, and therefore it is now lost.

https://web.archive.org/web/20130224020548/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/thumbnails.php?album=8 - album 8 has illustrations of 3886 from Komica. There are also his 5th and 3rd internet website birthday works that have way too many niche characters. You can find more about them with their Danbooru links (linked below). He is one of the only archived artists of Komica. Sadly, a lot of things from the early internet are gone, and the few 3886 arts remaining are on this Wayback Machine page. I did a blog post about 3886 because I managed to find the original thread on a dead Traditional Chinese wiki archive. Here are the links to the Danbooru posts of the birthday arts: https://danbooru.donmai.us/posts/297778 and https://danbooru.donmai.us/posts/735284 .

https://web.archive.org/web/20180219235340/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/thumbnails.php?album=9 - album 9 is a random illustration dump from the first "3rd year", with yukkuri (Touhou 2chan meme), to Pixiv uploads (we'll see that later, near the end), which are the most recent pre-2nd website artworks he has done.

- album 10 is gone, and we can't know anything about it. He made it in 2009, but it's hard to link stuff between the art gallery and the 1st website because the archive is a massive maze. I've seen works from this album, but I can't explain it.

- album 11 from 2009 had 6 files, but for the same reason as album 7, it is lost.

https://web.archive.org/web/20180220002227/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/thumbnails.php?album=12 - album 12 is the K-ON folder (2009). Those few fan-art pieces are pretty cute!

https://web.archive.org/web/20180220003053/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/coppermine/thumbnails.php?album=13 - album 13 has nothing interesting (speculative technology drawings). It is his "last" album in the gallery. To be honest, album 9 is where the last stuff actually is. And now, let's go somewhere more interesting....

https://web.archive.org/web/20070805124859/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/bblog/ https://web.archive.org/web/20090220182518/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/bblog/ https://web.archive.org/web/20110325193024/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/bblog/ - okay, so I'd rather start with his 2nd website, which is the blog, because it does not have as much content as the 1st one, and the 1st one was hosted during the same time (pre-gallery to Pixiv), except that this one ended way before Pixiv and is related to lots of guro_chan lost content, which is too hard to find now. On the blog, he actually wrote all his posts, so we can read his own words for the first time. You'll see the guro from 2007, when he started the blog, to his last posts after the abandonment of the blog, named "three years later" and "valentine" in 2013: https://web.archive.org/web/20160625133530/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/bblog/?postid=26

https://web.archive.org/web/20080512073402/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/wp/ - while he did all the commissions for people on imageboards, guro on bblog, etc... this is the website where literally everything is. In fact, the art gallery I've showcased was inside the 1st website. All his internet footprint except bblog and Pixiv is here on his old coded website from 2008 to 2013. Zenith said this site died from it when he did the gallery/first website return of 2018 before shutting it down with the following note: "Awakening from frozen sleep (?) When I look back at it, it's been a long time since I've been able to break through the net page (I've had a breakthrough in a few years). The webpage is not unexpectedly returned to the continuation of the meeting, but it is not yet renewed (excluding the original work and starting the editing again); Pixiv. https://pixiv.me/szenithlee" (https://web.archive.org/web/20180712020820/http://zenith.love-n-peace.org/wp/)

https://www.pixiv.net/en/users/861227 - the Pixiv account has his last works, 38 files up to 2018. His digital footprint ended in 2020 on Pixiv when he posted a picture of his desktop in his Pixiv biography.

I don't believe he died because it was only 6 years ago, or stopped because of the 4chan harasser, since he never cared about people being mad at him for guro art pieces (or futa pornography; he was treated as a troll for it on art websites), and his identity wasn't leaked. He probably stopped drawing in his life because he was tired of it. I did this archive of his internet history because I really enjoy all of his creations and mostly his style, which inspires me. My goal was to allow an easy navigation of his content for his other enjoyers and for my future audience to discover my main inspiration, as I have started art in late 2025. I'm writing this in February 2026, and I hope years from now my art will be as good as his art. Thank you for reading my post. My goal has now been achieved, and from now on, it's been a pleasure.
